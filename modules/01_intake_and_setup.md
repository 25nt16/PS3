MODULE 1 — Intake and Setup

Purpose:
Prepare and clean all inputs before summarization.

Steps:
1. Clean and normalize inputs (remove formatting errors, unify text encoding).
2. Standardize section names (e.g., “Intro” → “Introduction”).
3. Detect missing, empty, or very short sections.
4. If paper length exceeds safe processing limits, chunk text by section or logical subsections.
5. Prepare metadata:
   - section order
   - section length
   - flags for missing or short content
